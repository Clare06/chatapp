Account Access Recovery

We're here to help you regain access to your chat account securely. It appears that you're trying to log in from a different browser or device. To ensure the utmost security, we need to verify your identity.

Follow these steps to recover your account:

Step 1: Check Your Email

Open your email account associated with this chat application. You'll find an email from us titled "Private Key for Bubble Chat"

Step 2: Open the Email

Open the email and look for a message containing your private key. The subject should be "Private Key for Bubble Chat"

Step 3: Click the Activation Link

In the email, you'll find a link that says "Click the link to store" Click on this link. It will open a new browser window.

Important Note:

Please complete this process on the same device and browser you're currently using to access your email. Once activated, your private key will be associated with this browser for future logins.

If you have any difficulties or questions, please contact our support team at Support Email for assistance.

Thank you for using our chat application, and we apologize for any inconvenience. Your security is our top priority.
